FAQ

Common questions.

How long does Strategy First take?+
The engagement runs 4 weeks from kick-off to delivery. Week 1 is discovery, week 2 is the audit, week 3 is strategy build, and week 4 is delivery and walkthrough.
What does my involvement look like?+
You'll complete an intake form (about 45–60 minutes), join a 60–90 minute discovery call, and attend a 90-minute delivery walkthrough. Total time commitment: roughly 4–5 hours over 4 weeks. The research and build work happens on our end.
What format is the deliverable?+
You'll receive a complete Google Slides presentation — typically 80–120 slides across 10 sections — along with a separate 90-day execution calendar formatted for ClickUp, Asana, or a spreadsheet.
Can I continue working together after Strategy First?+
Yes — and most clients do. After Strategy First, many businesses continue with a monthly retainer (starting at $3,000/month) where I act as their Fractional CMO to help execute the strategy, manage their marketing team, and keep the plan moving.
Is this for B2B or B2C businesses?+
Both. Strategy First works well for home service businesses, local service companies, professional services firms, and B2B businesses in the $500K–$10M range. If you're unsure if it's the right fit, book a free call and we'll figure that out together.
What if I already have a marketing person or agency?+
Strategy First is designed to work alongside your existing team. It gives them the strategy, positioning, and direction they likely don't have — so they can execute more effectively. Many clients share the Strategy First deck directly with their agency or social media manager.
